Factors Influencing Heat Transfer Several factors impact the efficiency and effectivenes

Temperature Difference

The greater the temperature difference between the water body and the building, the more efficient the heat transfer. Effective heat exchange requires maintaining an optimal temperature differential to ensure consistent thermal performance.

Pipe Material and Insulation

The type of material used for pipes and their insulation affects heat transfer efficiency. Materials with high thermal conductivity, like copper, enhance heat transfer, while proper insulation reduces heat loss or gain during transit.

Flow Rate

The flow rate of water influences heat transfer efficiency. A higher flow rate generally improves heat transfer by increasing the amount of thermal energy carried from the water body to the building.
